# Automated Territory Battle Planner

## Roster Analysis

Scan the full guild roster to capture all available units and player statuses.
Ensure real‑time or scheduled sync with guild data sources.
Validate unit gear levels, star ratings, and key abilities for accurate planning.

## Inactive Player Filtering

Exclude inactive members based on a 1‑day inactivity threshold or an officer‑defined period.
Allow customizable criteria for marking players as inactive (e.g., no login, no raid participation).
Provide a toggle to temporarily include/exclude filtered players for scenario testing.

## Optimized Squad Assignment

Distribute available units across platoons/operations to maximize effectiveness.
Apply optimization rules by event type, target difficulty, and recommended team compositions.
Support manual overrides so officers can adjust assignments as needed.

## Outcome Prediction & Recommendations

Calculate potential results using existing recommended squads and current roster strength.
Generate pass/fail probability estimates for each operation based on unit power and synergy.
Deliver tailored recommendations for improving success rates (e.g., gear upgrades, alternative units).

# User‑Friendly Results Display

Present planning outcomes in a clear, actionable format (tables, charts, or heatmaps).
Highlight priority operations, bottlenecks, and underutilized units.
Offer export options (CSV, PDF) and shareable links for coordination among officers.

## Guides & Resources Hub

Include a dedicated section with instructional materials:
- Text guides for optimal TB strategies.
- Visual aids (flowcharts, unit placement diagrams).
- Links to video walkthroughs and community tutorials.
- Organize content by operation type, difficulty tier, and role (tank, healer, DPS, etc.).
